Device connects
Device is sent down to Device-Handler
Device-Handler adds it to know devices
---
Device-Handler wants to schedule device
Device-Handler asks Device for info
Device sends info
Device-Handler receives the data
Device-Handler schedules device
---
Device-Handler waits for the time the device should turn on
Device-Handler commands the device to turn on
--
Device-Handler waits for the time the device should turn off
Device-Handler commands the device to turn off
